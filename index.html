Retain your existing customers

Customer acquisition can be a difficult, time consuming and costly effort. Target and Connect With The Right Audience

Hard selling may get you a one time customer but connecting with people who already need your services will get you a willing customer. Even better, positioning yourself in the right place, where customers are already searching, will get the right customers flocking to you.

Build your brand online

Research shows that nearly all consumers, 97 percent, use some form of online media while researching products or services, even if the purchase is made offline, make sure what they find is drawing them in and keeping them interested.

Professional Representation You Can Rely On

Cut Costs Without Sacrificing Impact

Cut traditional marketing and customer acquisition costs without sacrificing impact by using technology to shift spending from more traditional vehicles like TV and print ads to digital and mobile advertising to more efficiently target consumers, allowing you to build better one-on-one personal relationships with every consumer.

Spot and capitalize on emerging trends with big data

Services

Full Service Social Media Management

We go beyond simply establishing your social media presence to develop, execute and manage your entire social strategy from start to finish. Content creation, regular updates, social customer service, contests & promotions, social marketing & much more, we formulate custom solutions that'll help you grow your business.

Responsive Web Design & Development

No more pinching, pushing and eventually abandoning your website as your customers struggle to read it on their smartphones. Responsive design & development by Fidelis Media lets your website appropriately render itself to fit the device it’s being viewed on. Layout, navigation, content, design and code all gracefully contract and expand to suit any device for one professional, seamless and easy to use experience.

Customized Strategy

No two businesses are exactly alike nor can they have the same exact goals. We work with you to draw up a customized plan for the content of the pages, the message you want to deliver and the frequency of updates. We also take into account what type of "voice" you'd like your business to convey, which helps keep a uniform feel among all of your social sites

Measure, Monitor & Track Impact

Fidelis Media monitors what your customers, competition and industry as a whole, are doing on social media and across the web. Using enterprise level data analytics, we can monitor and measure the impact of your brand as well as gather insights that will help drive your growth strategy.

Social Marketing

The “if you build it, they will come” approach to social media rarely works. The fact is, social media is a noisy space with more than one voice competing for your prospect’s attention. The solution? Effective and affordable social media advertising across major platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

Community Management

Too often, brands let questions, comments, and compliments via social channels go unanswered - or worse, they’re completely blindsided by what they find when they do finally tune in. Done right, social media community management can be labor intensive and time consuming. We do the heavy lifting so you can get out of the weeds and focus on the bigger picture: running your business

Training & Consulting

Sometimes you just want to do it yourself. That's why we offer customized social media training and consultation on community management, Facebook advertising, Pinterest, and LinkedIn marketing.

Search Engine Optimization

We optimize your website and published content to drive more visitors to your website and increase your ranking on search using industry best practices - which gets you ranked higher and seen by more people.
